Routes under `https://app.emergent.sh`. Routes not listed here redirect to
`/home` or are external (off-app) destinations served from `emergent.sh` or
`help.emergent.sh`.

## Core surfaces

| Route              | Surface                                  | Guide                                            |
|--------------------|------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| `/home`            | Home workspace with task form            | [Home](/guides/home)                             |
| `/chat`            | Live preview tab in the App builder      | [Live preview](/guides/preview)                  |
| `/register?ref=…`  | Referral-decorated registration surface  | [Refer and Earn](/guides/referrals)              |

## Legal and platform pages

| Route                 | Surface               | Guide                                    |
|-----------------------|-----------------------|------------------------------------------|
| `/ip-addresses`       | Static outbound IP list | [IP addresses](/guides/ip-addresses)   |
| `/terms-of-service`   | Terms of Service      | [Terms of Service](/guides/terms)        |
| `/privacy-policy`     | Privacy Policy        | [Privacy Policy](/guides/privacy)        |

## Modal-only surfaces

These do not have their own URL — they open as modal overlays on top of `/home`
but have dedicated documentation in the Guides tab:

| Trigger                                     | Modal                | Guide                                                 |
|---------------------------------------------|----------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Footer **Pricing** link / top-chrome **Buy Credits** | Plan selector     | [Pricing](/guides/pricing), [Buy credits](/guides/buy-credits) |
| Avatar menu → **Account Settings**          | Account settings     | [Account settings](/guides/account-settings)          |
| Avatar menu → **Language**                  | Language picker      | [Language](/guides/language)                          |
| Avatar menu → **Refer and Earn**            | Referral share       | [Refer and Earn](/guides/referrals)                   |
| Task input → gear icon                      | Advanced Controls    | [Advanced controls](/guides/task-advanced-controls)   |
| Advanced Controls → **Select MCP Tools**    | MCP picker           | [MCP tools](/guides/mcp-tools)                        |
| Workspace pill → **Create new project**     | Create a project     | [Create a project](/guides/create-project)            |
| App/Wingman switcher → **Wingman**          | Wingman surface      | [Wingman](/guides/wingman)                            |
