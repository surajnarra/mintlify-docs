## Overview

**Buy Credits** is a shortcut in the top-right chrome that opens the plan
selection modal. It's the fastest path to add credits without leaving the
current build tab — the modal is shared with the subscription flow, so
choosing Standard or Pro from here also subscribes the account.

## Top-up vs subscription

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Standalone top-up" icon="coin">
    Available to Standard and Pro subscribers from the same modal. Adds credits to the Top-up bucket in [Credit usage](/guides/credit-usage).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscription upgrade" icon="arrow-up">
    Clicking **Upgrade to Standard** or **Upgrade to Pro** from here starts a subscription rather than a one-off top-up.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## When to use Buy Credits

- Running low mid-build and want to finish without waiting for the monthly refresh.
- Experimenting with heavy tasks (e.g. Pro-tier Ultra thinking runs) beyond your plan allotment.
- Granting credits to a specific project — top-up credits can be tracked separately in Credit Usage.
