## Overview

**Credit Usage** is the rightmost tab in Account Settings. It combines a live
balance card — split across plan, top-up, and free credit sources — with a
filterable usage history that records every credit movement across your
projects.

## Balance breakdown

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total available credits" icon="coin">
    Aggregate of the three pools below — this is what the Buy Credits pill in the top chrome displays.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plan credits" icon="tag">
    Credits granted by the current subscription (e.g. `0/5` on the free tier). Refreshed every billing cycle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top-up credits" icon="plus">
    Credits purchased outside the subscription. Never expire on their own.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free credits" icon="gift">
    Promotional or referral credits — for example the `Welcome Credits` grant that ships with every new account (`10/10`).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Usage history filters

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a date range">
    Default is `Last week`. Widen the range to audit monthly spend or reconcile against receipts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by project">
    `All projects` by default. Scope the list to a single workspace to diagnose one project's spend pattern.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by type">
    `All types` shows both credits earned (grants, top-ups) and credits consumed (agent runs). Narrow to a specific type for focused auditing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Entries

Each row displays: the date, the credit source label (`Free credits`,
`Welcome Credits`, etc.), the project that earned or consumed them, and the
signed delta (`+10` for grants, `-N` for spend). Totals on the right reflect
credits used and running balance for the selected range.
