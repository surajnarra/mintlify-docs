## Overview

The avatar button in the top-right chrome opens the account menu, which
exposes quick actions (upgrade, referral, language) and a link into the
**Account Settings** modal. The modal groups configuration into five tabs, the
first of which shows your profile and current plan.

## Profile tab

The first tab shows the signed-in account's email, profile picture (used
publicly anywhere the product surfaces your identity), editable display name,
and the currently attached personal plan. An **Upgrade Plan** button in the
Personal Plan row routes to the Plans modal.
