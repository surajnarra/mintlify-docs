## Overview

Below the task form on `Home`, Emergent surfaces a curated gallery of builds
created by other users. Each card advertises a template (a full-stack app,
mobile app, or landing page) and exposes two actions: **Preview** opens the
rendered app in a side panel without consuming credits, and **Remix** forks
the template into a new tab scoped to your active project.

## Card actions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Preview" icon="eye">
    Renders the built application in a live iframe inside the App builder so you can evaluate UX and visual fidelity before remixing. Preview does not create a new project.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remix" icon="shuffle">
    Clones the template's prompt, container template, and configuration into your active project, opens a new tab, and routes to `/chat` where you can iterate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Categories

The Showcase mixes three build-mode categories to match the Home task tabs:

- **Landing Pages** — marketing sites (e.g. "Auramax headphone website", "Spiderman launch website").
- **Advanced Apps** — full-stack tools (e.g. "Ops manager", "Ocean research platform").
- **Mobile Apps** — mobile-first experiences (e.g. "Meditation app").
- **Personal Tools** — utility builds (e.g. "Location based memory app").

## Remix flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a card">
    Scroll until the desired template is in view. Each card includes a thumbnail, title, and category label (e.g. `Landing Pages`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview optionally">
    Click **Preview** to inspect the rendered app in a side panel. You can remix from there too.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remix">
    Click **Remix** to fork the template. Emergent creates a new tab and drops you into the generated preview.
  </Step>
</Steps>
