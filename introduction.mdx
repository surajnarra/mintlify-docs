# Welcome to Emergent

Emergent is an AI-powered platform that lets you build fully functional web and mobile applications through simple conversations. Describe what you want to build, and Emergent's AI agents will design, code, and deploy your application from start to finish.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="App Builder" icon="code">
    Build full-stack web apps, mobile apps, and landing pages by chatting with an AI agent. Choose from multiple AI models including Claude and GPT.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wingman" icon="robot">
    Your 24/7 personal AI assistant that connects to your tools and messaging apps. Available as a Tech Co-founder, Sales Lead, Content Marketer, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Showcase" icon="grid">
    Browse and remix community-built apps. Get inspired by landing pages, advanced apps, business tools, and mobile apps built by other users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deploy Instantly" icon="rocket">
    Deploy your apps with one click. Share them publicly or keep them private. Connect to GitHub for version control.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
