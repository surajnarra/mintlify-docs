# Credits & Billing

Emergent uses a credit-based system. Credits are consumed when AI agents build or modify your apps, and when using Wingman.

## Credit Types

Your credit balance is made up of three types:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Plan credits** | Credits included with your subscription plan, reset each billing cycle |
| **Top-up credits** | Additional credits purchased separately |
| **Free credits** | Bonus credits from sign-up, promotions, or referrals |

New accounts receive **10 free credits** as welcome credits.

## Viewing Your Balance

Check your credit balance in two ways:

1. **Profile dropdown** — Click your avatar to see your total balance at the top of the menu
2. **Credit Usage tab** — Open Account Settings and click **Credit Usage** for a detailed breakdown

## Usage History

The Credit Usage page shows a detailed log of all credit transactions:

- **Date** — When the credits were used or added
- **Credits used** — How many credits were consumed
- **Balance** — Running balance after the transaction
- **Type** — Whether it was a build, a Wingman interaction, a top-up, or free credits
- **Project** — Which project the credits were used in

### Filtering

Filter your usage history by:

- **Time period** — Last week, custom date range
- **Project** — All projects or a specific one
- **Type** — All types or a specific credit type

## Buying Credits

Click **Buy Credits** in the top navigation bar to purchase additional credits. Credits can be added at any time and do not expire.
