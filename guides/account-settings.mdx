# Account Settings

Access your account settings by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner and selecting **Account Settings**.

<img
  src="/images/account-settings.png"
  alt="Account settings"
/>

## Profile

The default tab shows your profile information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Email** | The email address linked to your account (read-only) |
| **Profile picture** | Your avatar displayed publicly across the platform |
| **Name** | Your display name — click the edit icon to change it |
| **Phone Number** | Add and verify a phone number to receive WhatsApp updates and alerts |
| **Personal Plan** | Shows your current plan (Free, Standard, or Pro) with an option to upgrade |

## Manage Agents

Create, edit, and manage custom AI agents tailored to your specific workflows.

<img
  src="/images/manage-agents.png"
  alt="Manage Agents"
/>

Agents are organized into three types:

- **Main agents** — Primary agents that handle your core tasks
- **Sub-agents** — Specialized agents that work under main agents
- **MCP Tools** — Model Context Protocol tools that extend agent capabilities

<Note>
  Custom agents require a **Pro** plan. Upgrade to create domain-specific agents with flexible prompts and customization.
</Note>

## Preferences

Customize how Emergent works for you.

<img
  src="/images/preferences.png"
  alt="Preferences"
/>

### Language

Select your preferred language. The interface supports multiple languages.

### Appearance

Choose your visual theme:

- **Light** — Light background with dark text
- **Dark** — Dark background with light text
- **Follow System** — Automatically matches your operating system's theme setting

### Auto-summarisation in Chat

When enabled, Emergent will automatically summarize conversations when the context fills up, allowing you to keep working in the same chat without losing context. This is enabled by default.
