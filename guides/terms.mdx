## Overview

The **Terms of Service** page at `/terms-of-service` contains the current
agreement between Emergent Labs Inc. and the account holder. It is the
authoritative source for acceptable use, billing, indemnification, and
intellectual property language covering work created on the platform.

  ![Terms of Service](/images/0029_terms_of_service.png)
</Frame>

## What the page covers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Acceptable use" icon="shield-check">
    Permitted and prohibited uses of the Emergent agentic platform, including restrictions on abusive content generation and automated scraping.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing & credits" icon="receipt">
    How credits are consumed, refunded, and expire. Subscription renewal terms and late payment language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intellectual property" icon="copyright">
    Ownership of code generated by the platform — generally the user retains rights to their prompts and outputs, subject to the limitations in the full document.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Indemnification" icon="gavel">
    Mutual responsibilities between Emergent Labs Inc. and the account holder, including model provider pass-through terms.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Finding the page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Footer link">
    Scroll to the bottom of any App page and click **Terms of Service** under the legal footer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direct URL">
    Navigate to `/terms-of-service` — the page is public and does not require authentication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to app">
    Use the **Return to Home** button in the top-right chrome of the Terms page to jump back into the App builder.
  </Step>
</Steps>
