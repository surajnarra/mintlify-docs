## Documentation conventions

- **Mod** denotes the platform modifier key: Command (⌘) on macOS, Control on Windows and Linux.
- Product nouns — `Project`, `Universal Key`, `Wingman`, `MCP`, `Remix` — are capitalized consistently to match the in-product copy.
- Code, identifiers, and routes are shown in `monospace`. Fenced blocks show IP addresses, container image references, and shell output verbatim.
- Screenshots are captured from the signed-in App surface at `app.emergent.sh` and reflect the default English locale.
