## Overview

The **Privacy Policy** page at `/privacy-policy` hosts the authoritative
privacy agreement from Emergent Labs Inc. It enumerates categories of
personal information collected, the legal basis for processing, and the
third-party processors involved in delivering the service.

## Data categories

- **Contact information** — first and last name, phone number, email address.
- **User-generated information** — code, projects, repository connections, metadata, other content that you provide.
- **Feedback or correspondence** — support interactions and product feedback.
- **Marketing information** — communication preferences and engagement data.
- **Payment information** — billing details handled via the third-party payment processor listed in the policy.

## Finding the page

- Use the **Privacy Policy** footer link on any App page, or navigate directly to `/privacy-policy`.
- The page is public and displays the last-updated date at the top.
