# Wingman

Wingman is Emergent's personal AI assistant that goes beyond app building. It connects to your messaging apps and productivity tools to help you with daily tasks — from drafting emails to managing your calendar to qualifying sales leads.

<img
  src="/images/wingman-onboard.png"
  alt="Wingman onboarding"
/>

## Getting Started with Wingman

Switch to Wingman mode by clicking **Wingman** in the top navigation bar. On your first visit, you'll choose a persona that defines how Wingman behaves.

### Personas

Choose a role that best fits your needs:

| Persona | Focus | Example Tasks |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| **Tech Co-founder** | Code, systems, and shipping | Architecture advice, code reviews, deployment help |
| **Sales Lead** | Leads, outreach, and pipeline | Find leads, write cold emails, qualify prospects |
| **Personal Assistant** | Travel, agendas, and planning | Book travel, manage agendas, organize tasks |
| **Content Marketer** | Posts, planning, and brand voice | Write LinkedIn posts, plan content weeks, maintain brand voice |
| **Executive Assistant** | Inbox, calendar, and follow-ups | Manage email, schedule meetings, track follow-ups |

Click **Let's Go** on any persona card to create your Wingman.

## The Wingman Chat

After selecting a persona, you'll enter the Wingman chat interface.

<img
  src="/images/wingman-chat.png"
  alt="Wingman chat interface"
/>

### Chat Tab

The main chat area where you interact with Wingman. Features include:

- **Text input** — Type messages to your Wingman
- **Attachment** — Upload files for context
- **Voice input** — Speak your requests using your microphone
- **Quick actions** — Pre-built prompts relevant to your persona appear as clickable buttons

**Example quick actions for a Sales Lead:**
- "Connect all tools and brief me"
- "Alert me when a big lead comes in"
- "Follow up on emails I haven't heard back on"

### Tasks Tab

View and manage tasks that Wingman is tracking for you. Wingman can create, update, and complete tasks based on your conversations.

| **WhatsApp** | Interact with Wingman via WhatsApp messages |

### Memory

Wingman remembers context from your conversations. The Memory section lets you view and manage what Wingman has learned about your preferences and workflows.

### Skills

Configure specific skills and capabilities that your Wingman can use.

### Permissions

Control what actions Wingman is allowed to take on your behalf across connected integrations.

### Settings

General configuration for your Wingman instance.

## Multiple Wingmen

You can create multiple Wingman instances with different personas. Use the navigation dots at the bottom of the Wingman screen to switch between them.

<Tip>
  Connect Wingman to your phone via Telegram or WhatsApp for truly 24/7 access — get briefings, alerts, and responses on the go.
