## Overview

Emergent's pricing modal — reachable from the **Pricing** footer link, the
**Upgrade** avatar menu item, and several upsell surfaces — offers two paid
tiers (`Standard` and `Pro`) plus a free tier that every account starts on.
Both paid plans grant a monthly credit allotment, with additional features
layered on each tier.

## Plan tiers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Standard" icon="star">
    `100 credits / month`. Marked **Best Value**. Includes Mobile App development, private project hosting, GitHub integration, Fork tasks, and the ability to buy additional credits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro" icon="crown">
    `750 credits / month`. Includes everything in Standard plus the E2 Agent, a 1M-token context window, advanced analytics, Ultra thinking, system prompt editing, and custom agents.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Monthly vs Annual

Toggle between `Monthly` and `Annually • Save 17%` at the top of the modal.
Annual billing charges upfront and discounts the effective monthly rate; the
savings badge updates dynamically (for example `Save ₹14,398` on Pro).

## Promo codes and upsells

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Have a promo code?" icon="ticket">
    Expands a field where promotional codes can be applied. Some campaigns auto-apply — the modal shows a `Discount auto applied` chip in that case.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upgrade shortcuts" icon="tag">
    Entry points include: footer **Pricing**, top-chrome **Buy Credits**, avatar menu **Upgrade**, Manage Agents **Upgrade to Pro**, Wingman locked-state prompt.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Us" icon="headset">
    Bottom-of-modal link routes to `help.emergent.sh` for enterprise quotes or custom arrangements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credit allotments" icon="coin">
    The credits/month line next to each plan is configurable — Standard defaults to 100, Pro to 750. Additional credits can be purchased through Buy Credits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Plan-gated features

Standard unlocks mobile app builds, private hosting, GitHub integration, fork
tasks, and credit top-ups. Pro adds the E2 Agent, a 1M context window,
advanced analytics, Ultra thinking, system prompt editing, and the ability to
build custom agents from Manage Agents.
