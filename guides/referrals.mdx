## Overview

**Refer and Earn** is Emergent's referral program, accessible from either the
gift icon in the top chrome or the **Refer and Earn $200** row in the avatar
menu. Sharing your personal invite link grants credits to both parties when
the referred account subscribes.

## Reward structure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Referee" icon="user-plus">
    Signs up through your link and gets **5 free credits** on account creation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Referrer" icon="sack-dollar">
    Earns **50 credits** when the referred account starts any paid subscription, capped at $200 worth of credits per year.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Progress tracker" icon="gauge">
    The modal shows a progress bar from `$0` to `$200` so you can see how close you are to the annual cap.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unique invite link" icon="link">
    Every account gets a personalized `app.emergent.sh/register?ref=...` URL with a single-click **Copy** action.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Share surfaces

The Refer tab exposes four social share shortcuts alongside the **Copy**
button:

- **X (Twitter)** — prefills a tweet with the invite URL.
- **LinkedIn** — opens a LinkedIn share dialog.
- **Email** — drafts an email using the default mail client.
- **WhatsApp** — deep-links to WhatsApp's web share endpoint.
