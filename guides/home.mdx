## Overview

`Home` is the default landing surface at `/home` and the first tab in the App
builder chrome. It pairs a marketing header ("Where ideas become reality")
with a fully configurable task form: project scope, build-mode selector, prompt
textarea, and submission row with model, visibility, voice, and template
controls. Below the form sits a scrollable Showcase of community-built apps and
a closing "Start building with Emergent today" CTA.

## Task input

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type or dictate">
    Use the textarea directly, or press the microphone button to dictate. Mouse focus activates the form without changing the build mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a suggestion">
    Click any chip (for example `My Counter Part`) to populate the textarea with a starter brief; the agent still expects you to refine the prompt before submission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set visibility">
    The **Public** pill toggles between Public and Private publishing. Public projects are eligible for the Showcase; Private projects require a Standard plan or above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the upward arrow to queue the task. A new preview tab opens in the App builder chrome.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Suggestion chips

Each build mode ships with three suggestion chips plus a Wingman Beta entry
point that preview the kinds of builds Emergent is tuned for. Clicking a chip
populates the textarea without submitting.
